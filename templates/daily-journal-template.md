## How to Use This Template

1. **Copy this template** at the start of each day
2. **Fill in sections** as you go (don't wait until end of day)
3. **Be honest** - this is for you, not for show
4. **Track metrics** - they show progress over time
5. **Review weekly** - look for patterns and improvements
6. **Keep it in Git** - commit your journal daily

**Pro Tips**:
- Set a timer at end of day to fill this out (15-20 minutes)
- The more detailed, the more valuable for future reference
- Use this to track what AI prompts work best
- Your future self will thank you for thorough documentation
